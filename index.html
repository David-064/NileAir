Egypt's Wing to the World

For over five decades, NileAir has carried the warmth of Egyptian hospitality to every corner of the globe — connecting civilizations, one flight at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 1971, NileAir emerged from Egypt's ambition to connect its ancient lands with the modern world. What began with a single route from Cairo to London has blossomed into a network of 85+ destinations across 6 continents.

We are more than an airline. We are ambassadors of Egyptian culture, bearers of 5,000 years of hospitality, and guardians of the promise that every journey should feel like arriving home.

Today, with a fleet of 84 aircraft and over 12,000 dedicated employees, NileAir continues its mission to be the bridge between Egypt and the world — carrying dreams aloft on wings of gold.
