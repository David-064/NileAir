Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common passenger questions

You can book a flight by visiting our Book page, entering your passenger details, and confirming your reservation. You can also call our hotline at +20 2 1234 5678.

Economy class passengers are allowed one carry-on bag (7 kg) and one checked bag (23 kg). Business class passengers receive 2 checked bags (23 kg each). Extra baggage fees start at 150 EGP per kilogram.

Yes. Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before departure. Changes can be made up to 24 hours before the flight for a 200 EGP fee. Contact our support team or visit the Contact page.

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before your domestic flight departure time. Check-in counters close 45 minutes before departure. Online check-in opens 24 hours before your flight.

Refundable tickets can be fully refunded up to 48 hours before departure. Non-refundable tickets may receive flight credit for future bookings. Please submit a refund request via the Contact page.

Complimentary Wi-Fi is available on our Airbus A320neo fleet. Boeing 737 MAX 8 aircraft offer Wi-Fi for a small fee of 50 EGP per session. The Embraer E190 regional jets do not currently offer Wi-Fi.

Egyptian citizens need a valid National ID card. Foreign nationals must carry their passport. All passengers must present the same identification used during booking at check-in.

Yes! Students with a valid university ID receive 20% off all domestic routes year-round. Check our Offers page for the latest student and seasonal deals.
